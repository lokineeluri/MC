COMING SOON

THE DIRECTOR

GENRE: CRIME / THRILLER

"Director" is a gripping short film that combines elements of ambition, mystery, and suspense. The story revolves around a passionate college freshman aspiring to become a director. However, his dreams take an unexpected turn when he stumbles upon a lifeless body on his film set. As the plot unravels, the film seamlessly incorporates crime and investigative elements, thrusting the protagonist into a whirlwind of unexpected events. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between reality and fiction blur, challenging the protagonist's aspirations and forcing him to confront the dark underbelly of the filmmaking world. "Director" captivates audiences with its suspenseful narrative, exploring the consequences of pursuing one's dreams in the face of unforeseen circumstances.
- written and directed by - S.A. MOIZ
