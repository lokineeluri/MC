LOTUS

GENRE: SYCHOLOGICAL DRAMA

"LOTUS" is a poignant micro short film that delves into the profound subject of suicide through a heartfelt conversation between a pencil and a book.Through their exchange, the film explores themes of loneliness, despair, and the power of connection. With minimalist visuals and powerful dialogue, "LOTUS" invites viewers to reflect on the importance of reaching out, finding solace in human connection, and the potential to heal even the deepest wounds. It serves as a reminder of the value of empathy and compassion in the face of darkness.
- written and directed by - NAREN AND BHASHIT
