MIRROR MAZE

GENRE: SCI DRAMA

"Mirror Maze" is a captivating short film that delves into the transformative power of human interaction. The story follows a misanthropic man who despises social interaction but finds himself thrust into a mirror world devoid of humans. As he navigates this surreal landscape, he discovers a newfound sense of solitude and self-reflection. However, as time passes, he begins to yearn for genuine connection. When he returns to the real world, he realizes the value of human interaction and the impact it can have on his life. Through its thought-provoking narrative, "Mirror Maze" explores the profound importance of embracing human connections and the transformative potential they hold.
- written and directed by - JYOTEESH KUMAR
