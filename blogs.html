Art of Storyboarding :

Storyboarding is a visual planning tool used in filmmaking to outline the sequence of shots and visualize the director's vision. It involves creating a series of sketches or illustrations that depict key moments, camera angles, and composition of each scene in a film. Storyboards serve several purposes: they help filmmakers communicate their ideas to the cast and crew, ensure consistency in visual storytelling, and serve as a blueprint for the entire production process
